The Irreplaceable Designer

Crafting original concepts in a world of copying and AI.

Last week, I was at PLACES by Echosystem, an event I had the privilege of branding from inception to execution. Standing in that room, one of the major things that stood out amid the many compliments was how entirely unconventional, original, and unbelievable the design concepts were.

And that room is the exact inspiration for this class.

I strongly believe the average designer will get replaced by AI. Not because they aren’t good enough, but because they lack taste and a sense of originality. Their work will be replicated faster than they can churn it out.

That’s why I want to build a set of irreplaceable designers with great taste and impeccable execution. Over these 3 days, I’m showing you the exact framework I use to build visual systems that stand out in real spaces.

The 3-Day Syllabus

Day 01

The Spirit of Design

Laying down the foundational design psychology, core color theories, and alternative baseline thinking patterns required to generate unique visual systems from pure intuition.

Day 02

The Execution & Technical Workflow

A deep, practical breakdown in Photoshop. Watch the live construction of an entire asset campaign containing 3 to 5 coherent design concepts built completely from scratch.

Day 03

The Business of Strategy

How to turn your design output into client revenue. We will map out critical professional client agreements, onboarding documents, and premium agency workflows.
